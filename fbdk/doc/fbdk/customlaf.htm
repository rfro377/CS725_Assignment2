Configurable Look and Feel

You can use the lookAndFeel setting in the Preferences Dialog to select from the available Look and Feel configurations. These depend on the particular platform and Java™ Runtime Environment. Some of the available Look and Feel names and their corresponding FBDK appearance are as shown below.

Metal - The standard cross-platform Java Look and Feel

Nimbus - The newest Look and Feel

Windows - A Windows™ 95/98/NT/2000 Look and Feel

Windows Classic - A Windows 95/98/ME/NT/2000 Look and Feel

CDE/Motif - Common Desktop Environment Look and Feel
