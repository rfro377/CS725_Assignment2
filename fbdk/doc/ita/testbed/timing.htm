Timing Information

  1. The chain drive speed is such that a piece passes through the sortation station approximately every 6 seconds.
  2. The belt conveyor speed is such that the time following successful assembly from the first negation of the ASSEMBLY HOPPER FULL signal to the assertion of the RING ASSEMBLED signal is approximately 4.5 seconds.
