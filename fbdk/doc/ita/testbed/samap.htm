Sensor and Actuator Interfaces

Sensor signals are made available at a 25-pin D-shell socket. They are compatible with 24VDC current-sinking input circuits and are packed into a 16-bit WORD for transmission to the controller.

Actuator signals are made available at a 15-pin D-shell plug. They are compatible with 24VDC current-sourcing output circuits and are packed into a 16-bit WORD for transmission from the controller.

As shown below, switches are inserted in some of the signal lines to facilitate the testing of error detection, diagnosis and recovery algorithms.
