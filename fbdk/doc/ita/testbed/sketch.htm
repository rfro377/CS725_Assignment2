1. Sketch the system

In the testbed used in the IEC 61499 feasibility demonstration at the HMS International Symposium in Kitakyushu City, Japan on October 19-20 , 2000, a yellow plastic ring is assembled to an aluminum peg. As shown below, components may be placed on a chain conveyor in an arbitrary order and are sorted into appropriate positions for assembly on a belt conveyor. Unassembled parts are rejected at the end of the belt conveyor.

Power requirements are 24VDC, fused on-board at 3A.

The testbed is mounted to a base whose dimensions are approximately 1m x 50 cm. The base has a latching cover with wheels and handles for transport. The dimensions of the base and cover are approximately 1m x 50cm x 50cm when assembled and latched. The weight of the combined testbed and cover is approximately 40 kg.
