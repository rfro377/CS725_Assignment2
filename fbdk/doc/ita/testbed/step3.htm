s3. Ring in Chute [s1] [s2] [s4] [s5] [s6] [s7]

After the ring has been deflected by the sort solenoid, it rolls into the chute and is held in place by the index cam, where its presence can be detected.

The chute can hold up to five rings; however, the larger the number of rings in the chute, the higher the chance that operation of the rotary solenoid will deliver more than one ring into the assembly hopper [s4].
