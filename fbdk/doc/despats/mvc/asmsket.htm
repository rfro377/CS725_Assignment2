1. Sketch the system

 Two cylinders (CYL1 and CYL2) flanged to one another transport a workpiece from position HOME to MID. At MID, a second part is press fit to the workpiece and the system then moves to END. Here, the assembled workpiece is removed and the system then moves back to position HOME. The positions of the workpiece are given by the boolean expressions
HOME = HOME1 & HOME2
MID = END1 & HOME2
END = END1 & END2 .
