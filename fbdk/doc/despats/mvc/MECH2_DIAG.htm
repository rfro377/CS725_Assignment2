FUNCTION_BLOCK MECH2_DIAG

An instance of this function block type utilizes two instances of the MECH1_DIAG type to provide diagnostics for a mechanism modeled by the MECH2_MDL type or the equivalent physical device. Faults detected are timeouts when the mechanism is advancing (moving in the FWD direction) or retracting (moving in the REV direction). The corresponding timeout periods and diagnostic messages are given by the NF/MSGF and NR/MSGR pairs, respectively.
