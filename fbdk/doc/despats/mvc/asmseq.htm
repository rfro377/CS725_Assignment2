3. Animate operational sequences

The ASM_VIEWL configuration is used to produce an animation of the desired press-fit operational sequence using the dual-stroke cylinder for transport of the original and assembled workpiece.

0. Both cylinders retracted,
waiting for PART1.

1. PART1 loaded,
CYL1 advances.

2. CYL1 advanced,
waiting for PART2.

3. PART2 loaded,
assembly begins.

4. Assembly completed.

5. CYL2 advances,
press retracts.

6. CYL2 advanced, waiting
for part to be unloaded.

7. Assembled part unloaded,
both cylinders retract.

0. Retraction complete,
waiting for another PART1.
