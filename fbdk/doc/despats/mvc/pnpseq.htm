3. Animate operational sequences

The PNP_VIEWL configuration is used to produce an animation of the desired sequence of operation of the pick and place mechanism.

0. Mechanism is at home position, waiting for the new workpiece which is advancing along the conveyor. 1. The workpiece has arrived at the end of the conveyor. 2. The workpiece is loaded to the mechanism by activating the electromagnet. 3. The mechanism has advanced to the end of the x-axis, and a new workpiece has been loaded onto the conveyor.
4. The mechanism has advanced to the end of the y-axis, and the new workpiece has advanced to the end of the conveyor. 5. The workpiece is unloaded onto the worktable by de-activating the electromagnet. 6. The mechanism is returning directly to the situation of step 1 by driving both motors in reverse simultaneously.
